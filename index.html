Facts about Llamas that you can't prove aren't true

  • Llamas can literally shoot lasers out of their eyes, but without glasses to magnify the lasers, they are imperceptible to the human eye.
  • Llamas can get up to 32 miles to the gallon in the city and up to 48 miles to the gallon on a standard Peruvian Highway.
  • Llama wool is fire resistant. Have you ever seen a llama on fire?
  • Llamas can be found on at least 3.5 continents spread across at least 1 world.
  • It's physically impossible to get a llama to listen to all the tracks on an album in order. This means they will never be able to fully appreciate The Avalanche's album Since I Left You and this makes me sad.
